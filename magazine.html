Summer 2021 | Issue No. 1 Our first magazine issue features insightful articles and beautiful art works by our team, as well as submissions from other students who are passionate about mental health. The articles in this magazine cover everything from self help to the need for sleep to the representation of mental health in the media. We hope you enjoy reading our magazine, and find it educational and comforting.
