What It Means to Love Your Life

What It Means to Love Your Life

Jul 21, 2022 | 2 min | Chelsie Napiza



What’s the first thing that goes through your mind when you wake up in the morning?

The answer may differ from one person to another, but we all probably desire one thing-- the hope that today will be kind and that life will be good to us. But here’s the thing about wanting something from the universe: we need to work for it. First, we have to put in the effort before actually going through with it with the help of a manifestation ritual.

Doing so will allow you to follow through.

Now, loving your life doesn’t necessarily mean you have to fill it with nice things, even though you can feel free to do so if you have the means. What I want you to understand is that, if you love your life or if you are on a quest to be somewhere, you have to start now.

The step does not have to be this huge declaration for everybody to see. It could be as easy as writing an affirmation on a post-it note and placing it in a certain spot that you’ll always see. It could be making your own coffee the exact way you like it, then taking a minute out of your busy day to savor the flavor and thank yourself for doing such an amazing job.

It does not matter what kind of act you do today, so long as the intention is there. You are allowed to love your life because you only get one chance at living it. Every moment, every second that passes, is something none of us can ever take back. So, breathe. Breathe and let yourself marvel at how life can be complex, chaotic, and utterly confusing -- but at the same time, it is the most stellar and magnificent experience one can be blessed to have.

I promise you this: one day, you’ll wake up and be filled with warmth, love, and gratitude… and the first thing that will rush through your mind is your undeniable love for yourself and life itself.

If that day isn’t today, there’s always tomorrow. We can try again together.
