Our Mission

Who Are We?

Café Mocha is a student-led non-profit organization dedicated to mental health advocacy. Our goal is to increase awareness and erase the stigma around mental health, while providing opportunities to other students.

    Our Goals

  • Create a magazine that addresses topics on mental health and current events, featuring creative work
  • Provide support and opportunities for students, including mental health resources and an educational/fun magazine
  • Connect and empower students by amplifying resources and offering a place for artistic expression
